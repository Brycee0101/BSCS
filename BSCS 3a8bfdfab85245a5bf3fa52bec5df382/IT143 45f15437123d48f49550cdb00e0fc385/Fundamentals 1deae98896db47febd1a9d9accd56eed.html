a multidisciplinary field of study focusing on the design of computer systems, programs or applications particularly the interaction between the users and computers.

aims to allow people to execute tasks:

Usability

factors to consider:

  • ease of learning
  • high speed of task performance
  • low user error rate
  • subjective user satisfaction
  • user retention over time
